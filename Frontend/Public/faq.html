Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common SmartSpend questions.

How do I create a SmartSpend account?
Click the “Sign Up” button on the login page, enter your details, and confirm your email to activate your account.
Can I import expenses from a spreadsheet?
Yes! Go to the Upload page and select “Import CSV/Excel” to automatically add your expenses into SmartSpend.
How do I adjust my monthly budget?
Navigate to the Budget page, edit your desired category’s limit, and click “Save Changes” to update your budget.
Is my financial data secure?
SmartSpend uses encrypted connections and secure data storage to keep your personal and financial information safe.
Who can I contact for additional help?
You can reach our support team anytime via the “Contact Support” page for personalized assistance.
